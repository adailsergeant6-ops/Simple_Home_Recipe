Cooking Tips

# Cooking Tip Description
1 Read the Recipe First Always read the full recipe before starting to avoid mistakes.
2 Keep Your Knives Sharp Sharp knives make cutting easier, faster, and safer.
3 Prep Ingredients First Measure and chop ingredients before cooking for smoother preparation.
4 Season as You Go Add seasoning gradually to improve flavour balance.
5 Avoid Overcrowding the Pan Leave space between food so it cooks evenly.
6 Taste While Cooking Adjust seasoning during cooking for better results.
7 Clean as You Cook Keeping your workspace tidy saves time after cooking.
