The Simple Home Recipes Story

Making cooking accessible, simple, and joyful for everyone

We believe that everyone deserves access to easy-to-follow recipes and cooking guidance. Simple Home Recipes is an online platform dedicated to breaking down the barriers between people and the kitchen. Whether you're a student learning to cook for the first time, a busy professional seeking quick solutions, or simply someone who loves exploring new dishes—this platform is designed for you.

Our Beginning

2026

Established in 2026 as an academic project, Simple Home Recipes emerged from a simple observation: many people avoid cooking because they find recipes overwhelming, intimidating, or time-consuming. We saw an opportunity to create something different—a platform where recipes are stripped down to their essence, organized by meal type (breakfast, lunch, and desserts), and presented in a way that anyone can understand.

What We Offer

Simple Home Recipes is a strictly online platform that provides:

  • Easy-to-follow recipes categorized by meal type
  • Quick cooking guides for students and beginners
  • Structured navigation for discovering new dishes
  • Practical cooking tips and techniques
  • A supportive community focused on learning

We don't sell products—we provide knowledge, guidance, and inspiration.

Our Mission

To provide accessible and beginner-friendly cooking recipes that empower students, busy professionals, and home cooks to prepare delicious meals with confidence.

Our Vision

To become a trusted digital source for simple home cooking guidance, where anyone—regardless of their cooking experience—can find the recipes and knowledge they need to create meals they're proud of.

Our Goals

  • Make cooking simple, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone
  • Build a comprehensive library of well-organized recipes
  • Create educational content that demystifies cooking techniques
  • Foster a community where people share their cooking experiences
  • Continuously improve the platform based on user feedback

Looking Ahead

The development of this platform strengthens our digital presence by organizing recipes into structured categories for easy navigation. In the future, we envision expanding to include:

  • Video tutorials demonstrating cooking techniques
  • User accounts for personalized recipe collections
  • Interactive features for sharing feedback and reviews
  • Advanced search and filtering capabilities
  • Community challenges and cooking events
